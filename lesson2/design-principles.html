3 Noteworthy Principles of Design

White Space and Clean Design

Arium Botanical

This site is a good example of White space and clean design. They utilize the white space and make the focus the content they provide and their products. No nonsense. My kind of website.

Proximity

Greta Thurnberg

I like how this site is quite minimal and does not have things in conventional places but uses the Principle of Proximity (and visual Hierarchy) to show viewers whats important. Especially using the girls name behind the figure helps your brain make the the connection who that figure is compared to if it did not have the wording behind it.

Contrast

Yamaha Motorsports

Yamaha Motorsports was one of the websites I found that used a black background and contrasting colors for the wording. As well as the contrast of the light blue swirl in the photo, it uses contrast in that compostion to emphasize their message.
